Incident Description

During routine lifting operations, operator noticed a sudden pressure drop in the hydraulic system. The crane was unable to maintain lifting pressure and had to be immediately shut down. Visual inspection revealed hydraulic fluid leaking from the main pump assembly. The pressure gauge was fluctuating between 1500-2000 PSI (normal operating pressure should be stable at 3200 PSI). No unusual sounds or vibrations were reported prior to the failure.
