Task Details

Motor Bearing Replacement

Replace main motor bearings showing excessive vibration. Readings indicate wear beyond acceptable limits. Use new sealed bearings (part #MBR-75A) and ensure proper alignment during installation.

Parts Required

Part Number Description Quantity Status
MBR-75A Sealed Motor Bearing 2 Available
SL-102 Bearing Sealant 1 Available
GSK-45 Motor Gasket 1 Ordered

Work Instructions

  1. Lock out/tag out the crane power supply
  2. Remove motor access panel
  3. Disconnect wiring harness (document connection points)
  4. Remove motor mounting bolts and extract motor
  5. Open motor housing and remove old bearings
  6. Clean bearing housings thoroughly
  7. Install new bearings with proper alignment tools
  8. Apply sealant and replace gaskets
  9. Reassemble motor and mount to crane
  10. Reconnect wiring harness
  11. Test motor operation before returning to service
  12. Document all work performed and parts used

Safety Requirements

  • Personal Protective Equipment (hard hat, safety glasses, gloves)
  • Fall protection when working at heights
  • Proper lifting techniques for motor handling
  • Lockout/tagout procedures must be followed
  • Work area must be cordoned off with safety barriers
